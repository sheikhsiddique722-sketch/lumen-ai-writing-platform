Questions

Things people ask before signing in.

Does Lumen really learn my voice, or is it a marketing phrase?
It learns. Lumen builds a private voice model from documents you choose — typically your last 5,000 to 10,000 words. The model is yours alone, never used to train anything else, and you can wipe it at any time from settings.
Where does my writing live, and who can see it?
Encrypted at rest on SOC2 Type II infrastructure, in the region you select (US, EU, or APAC). Your drafts are never used for model training. Only people you explicitly invite to a document can see it.
Can I export everything? I don't like lock-in.
Always. Markdown, DOCX, PDF, or a clean JSON archive of your entire workspace including version history. One click, no questions.
What does Lumen not do?
It doesn't autopilot. Lumen will never publish, send, or finalize your work without you. We believe AI should hand the pen back, not keep it.
Which models power Lumen, and can I choose?
Lumen runs on a routed mix of leading frontier models, blended with our own voice-adaptation layer. On Author and Studio plans, you can pin a specific model per document if you prefer the texture of one over another.
Is there a student or non-profit plan?
Yes — 60% off Author for verified students, and a free Studio tier for registered non-profits under 25 seats. Reach out from a school or org email.
